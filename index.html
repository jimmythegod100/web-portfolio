FAQ

Common questions

How long does a typical project take?
Starter sites launch in 5–7 days. Business sites take 10–14 days. E-commerce stores run 14–21 days depending on catalog size.
Do I need to provide content and images?
Yes — your logo, text, and brand photos work best. I can use professional placeholders during design and help source stock imagery if needed.
Will I own the website?
Absolutely. You get full access, all source files, and no lock-in. The site is 100% yours after launch.
Do you handle hosting?
Yes. I set up hosting on Netlify, Vercel, or your preferred provider and walk you through managing it.
What about changes after launch?
I offer monthly maintenance packages, or you can hire me for one-off updates anytime. Most clients send me content changes and I handle the rest.
